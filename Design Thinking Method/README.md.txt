# Open Materials for the Study

This repository provides supporting materials for the conceptual study
“Innovating Access to Digital Education Using the Design Thinking Method
through the Bridge International Academies Case Study as a Strategy to Reach SDG 4”.

The study is based on qualitative desk research and secondary sources.
No primary dataset was generated.

## Repository Contents
- data_mapping.pdf: Mapping of literature and case study insights to Design Thinking stages
- conceptual_framework.png: Adapted Design Thinking framework for Indonesia
- methodology_notes.txt: Summary of the analytical approach

## Reproducibility Note
These materials document the analytical logic used in the study and are
provided to support transparency and conceptual reproducibility.
